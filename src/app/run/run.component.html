Running Your First Ionic Project

We will run/serve the application on the application server and display it on a browser

In this scenario you are probably wondering to yourself: "I have created my first application so what???". Well fear not I will guide you through this. You have created your first project but what do you do with it you may ask... Fear Not i will show you

Step 1: Once we have created our project(if you havent go to this page) we are going to go to our terminal and we are going to run this command:

ionic serve

Step 2: We are going to wait for a bit to transpile and then we will wait for the browser to open annnnnd... we're done!!!

A little sidenote: From then you can open the created folder in using your favourite text browser and conquer the world!!!
